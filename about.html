Canadian Charity that aims to Eradicate Illiteracy, Hunger and Disease in Rural Africa
WorldLove24 runs over 3,000 schools that help educate 8.4 million children in tribal and rural areas of Africa. We also provide healthcare and food to impoverished and helpless children in rural Africa.
About WorldLove24
WorldLove24 Foundation of Canada was founded in 2007. WorldLove24 is a registered Canadian charity and a non-profit organization engaged in the education of tribal, indigenous children in rural Africa. WorldLove24 has ZERO administrative cost. WorldLove24 In Canada

We welcome donors as well as volunteers to join us in this noble cause. Volunteer registration is open. We have regional chapters across Canada in Winnipeg, Greater Toronto Area (GTA), Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, London, Windsor, Ottawa etc.

WorldLove24 is primarily supported by individuals which perhaps makes it a largest individual donation based movement in the world.

The idea of WorldLove24 is to eradicate suffering and from rural areas in Africa. The individual donors are from all ver the world. Apart from individual donors, WorldLove24 is also supported by businesses and by different charitable foundations across the globe to carry forward their objective of taking education at grass-roots.
